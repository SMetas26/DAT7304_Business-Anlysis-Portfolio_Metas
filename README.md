Hotel Booking Demand Analysis and Forecasting
Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive data analytics study of hotel booking demand and customer cancellation behaviour for Canaves Collection, a hospitality organisation operating in a highly competitive and seasonal industry.
The objective of the project is to explore historical booking data, identify customer behaviour patterns, forecast future demand, and develop interactive business intelligence dashboards to support managerial decision-making.
The project combines Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), Time Series Analysis, Forecasting Models, and Power BI Dashboarding to transform raw hotel reservation data into actionable business insights.

Business Problem

The hospitality industry faces challenges related to:

Seasonal fluctuations in booking demand
High cancellation rates
Dependence on external booking channels
Revenue optimization and capacity planning
This project investigates these challenges and demonstrates how data analytics can support strategic and operational decisions.
